About Bhooyam

At Bhooyam, our mission is to empower farmers with smart, data-driven solutions that optimize resources, improve productivity, and promote sustainable agriculture.

Our Vision

We envision a future where smart farming solutions are accessible to farms of all sizes, contributing to a more sustainable and food-secure world.

Our Mission

Our mission is to integrate cutting-edge technology with traditional farming practices, creating a more efficient and resilient agricultural ecosystem.

Our Story

Bhooyam was founded to address the challenges faced by farmers in water use, inconsistent irrigation, and unpredictable weather patterns. Through our Smart Agri Monitoring System prototype, we have brought modern technology to traditional farming, improving resource management and crop productivity.

Our Team

Our team comprises experts from agriculture and technology sectors. Agronomy specialists bring decades of farming wisdom, while engineers innovate with IoT and AI to provide smart solutions.

Achievements & Milestones

  • Developed a prototype for automated irrigation in hydroponics.
  • Partnered with local farming communities to refine our solutions.
  • Honored in various agritech forums for resource optimization.
  • Secured funding for expanding our R&D efforts.

How It Works

Step 1: Collect Data

Step 2: Analyze

AI analyzes the data to identify crop needs.

Step 3: Take Action

Automated irrigation and nutrition supply based on analysis.
